About Eventify

Eventify is an all-in-one event operations platform that helps you manage registrations, QR attendance, certificates, and analytics—all from one clean workspace. Our mission is to empower organizers and communities to create impactful events with ease.

Our Journey

2024 Founded

Eventify was founded by Shashank Pandey with a vision to simplify event management for communities and organizations.

2025 First Major Release

Launched the first version of Eventify, enabling QR attendance and certificate automation for college fests and community events.

2026 Growth & Expansion

Expanded features, improved analytics, and reached hundreds of organizers and volunteers across India.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Eventify free?

Yes! Eventify offers a free forever plan for individuals and communities.

Can I track attendance with QR codes?

Absolutely. QR-based attendance is built-in for fast, contactless check-ins.

How do I issue certificates?

Certificates are generated automatically for participants after events.
